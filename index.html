What is the wonders of worlds?

The term "Wonders of the World" generally refers to a list of extraordinary and impressive human-made structures or natural phenomena that are considered remarkable for their beauty, significance, or historical importance. These wonders capture the imagination and awe of people around the world. The selection process and acceptance, however, vary depending on the specific list.
